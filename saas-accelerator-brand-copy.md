## Copy Notes (internal, not published)

**v2 changes from v1:**

1. **Modeled after top Skool communities.** Studied Evolve, Origins, CoderCo, KubeCraft, Vibe Coding Academy, AI Automation Society. Applied their patterns: bullet-heavy, scannable, proof upfront, clear "What You Get" lists.
2. **Added 1-on-1 and group coaching.** Replaced vague "live build sessions" with concrete offers: 1-on-1 coaching, weekly group coaching calls (recorded). Matches CoderCo (7+ live calls/week), KubeCraft (mentorship calls), Evolve (group calls with founders).
3. **Added WhatsApp direct link.** Replaces "DM us directly." Lower friction, more personal. Matches "Have questions? Reach out" pattern from CoderCo and Origins.
4. **Simplified annual upsell.** 1-on-1 strategy session + 2 months free. Clean, concrete, no fluff.
5. **Shortened About section.** Fits Skool's ~1000 char limit. Bullet-heavy format instead of paragraphs.
6. **Removed "not a course" repetition.** Said once, clearly, moved on. Over-repeating it made it sound defensive.
7. **Pricing: $47/mo, $470/yr.** Lower barrier matches positioning as an accessible community for first-time founders, not a premium mastermind.

2. No course structure. Access + coaching + curated intel. Nothing to "complete" and cancel.

4. Data-backed claims. Micro SaaS growing 30%/yr. Solo founders $5K-$50K/mo. 70%+ margins. MVPs under $1K.
5. High-ticket offer: Build Sprint ($2-3K, 4 weeks, done-with-you).
6. Tool-agnostic framing. Claude Code, Cursor, Replit mentioned. Community survives tool changes.
7. Price lock preserved from Claudify.
