# Comparison Page Template (vs Competitor)

> **Purpose:** Fair comparison page to capture "brand vs competitor" queries
> **Min Words:** 600
> **Key Rule:** Be factual and fair, include "when to choose them" section

---

## Meta Tags

**Title (60 chars max):**
```
[Brand] vs [Competitor]: Honest Comparison [Year]
```

**Meta Description (155 chars max):**
```
[Brand] vs [Competitor] compared. See pricing, features, and which is right for you. Fair analysis from [Brand].
```

---

[Brand] and [Competitor] are both [category] providers serving [audience]. [Brand], based in [location], focuses on [key differentiator]. [Competitor] is known for [their strength]. This comparison helps you choose the right option for your needs.
