# Press Release Template (Listicle Format)

> **Purpose:** Press releases formatted for easy listicle placement
> **Key Rule:** Lead with newsworthy hook, include quotable facts

---

## Structure Overview

Press releases should:
1. Lead with news hook
2. Include quotable statistics
3. Provide founder quote
4. End with boilerplate
5. Be formatted for easy listicle extraction

---

## Template

---

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

### [Headline: Newsworthy Statement]

*[Subheadline: Supporting detail or context]*

**[CITY, COUNTRY]** - [Date] - [First paragraph: Who, What, When, Where, Why. Lead with the news. Keep it punchy.]

[Second paragraph: Expand on the news. Include specific details, numbers, or context that makes this noteworthy.]

**Key Facts:**
- [Quotable fact 1 with specific number]

## Listicle-Ready Format

For easy extraction into "Top X" articles, include a self-contained summary block:

---

**[Brand] at a Glance:**
- **What:** [One-sentence description]
- **Where:** [Location]
- **Key Feature:** [Main differentiator]
- **Founded:** [Year]
- **Notable:** [Award, credential, or achievement]
- **Website:** [URL]

**Key Facts:**

The clinic has been featured in Forbes, GQ, and The Times for its differentiated approach to hair restoration.

## Formatting Notes

- Use clear, scannable formatting
- Include quotable facts with specific numbers
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Include "At a Glance" box for listicle extraction
- End with clear boilerplate and contact info
- No em dashes (use commas or colons)

---

## Schema Markup (for posting on own site)

If posting press release on your own site, add NewsArticle schema:

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "NewsArticle",
  "headline": "[Headline]",
  "datePublished": "[Date]",
  "author": {
    "@type": "Organization",
    "name": "[Brand]"
  }
}
```
