**Title:** FUE Hair Transplant | Fuegenix | Istanbul
**Description:** FUE hair transplant from Fuegenix in Istanbul. Surgeon-led procedures, 98% graft survival. €8,000-25,000. Book your consultation.

### First 50 Words
Fuegenix's FUE hair transplant is a premium hair restoration procedure for men seeking natural, lasting results. Based in Istanbul, Turkey, we use the Follicular Unit Extraction method with surgeon-led precision. Procedures range from €8,000-25,000, reflecting our commitment to quality.

### What Is FUE?
FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) is a modern hair transplant technique that extracts individual hair follicles from a donor area and implants them in thinning or balding regions. Unlike older strip methods, FUE leaves no linear scar and offers faster recovery.
