## THE HEADLINE FINDINGS

### 1. Two distinct competitor categories emerged

| Category | Competitors | What they sell |

| **Content autopilot** (generate + publish) | AutoSEO, RankPill, BabyLoveGrowth, Outrank | "We write 30 articles/month and publish them for you" |

| **Traditional SEO tools** | HikeSEO, Soogle, Climbo, Bazoom, RankingMasters | SEO automation, backlinks, reviews (not AI-search focused) |

### 2. MentionedIn.ai sits between both categories
None of the content autopilots do proper tracking. None of the trackers give you implementation actions. We do both.

### 3. Only 3 competitors offer white-label for agencies
Briljant (EUR39/mo), HikeSEO ($90-749/mo), Climbo ($1,497-$2,997 lifetime). Massive gap.

### 4. Instagram ads = proven demand
14 companies spending money on IG ads to acquire customers. The market is real and growing.

## WHAT MentionedIn.ai HAS THAT NOBODY ELSE DOES

### 1. 10-Run Consistency Testing
Every competitor runs a query 1 time. We run it 10 times per LLM and report statistical mention rate (7/10 = 70%). This is scientifically rigorous. Nobody else does this.

### 2. Audit-to-Playbook Engine
Nimt.ai shows you the problem. PromptWatch shows you citations. BabyLoveGrowth generates articles. NONE of them say "here's exactly what to change on YOUR website, URL by URL." We do.

### 3. Battle Cards for Missing Queries
When a brand isn't mentioned for a query, we generate specific fix instructions: what content to create, which entities to include, which sources to get cited in, which technical changes are needed.

### 4. First 50 Words Technical Audit
Automated check: does every key page have WHO/WHAT/WHERE/PRICE in first 50 words? Plus robots.txt AI crawler check, SSR/SSG verification, schema markup validation. Zero competitors do this.

### 5. Affordable White-Label Per-Org Pricing
$149/mo for up to 15 clients (Agency tier). Not per-client or per-site — per-org with client seat limits. Briljant charges per site (EUR39-99). HikeSEO charges per site ($90-749). MentionedIn.ai's flat tier pricing scales much better for agencies with many clients.

### 6. "Business in a Box" for Beginners
No competitor targets complete beginners. HikeSEO targets "small agencies with little SEO expertise" but still assumes they HAVE an agency. We target people who want to START an agency. Methodology + tool + community + client acquisition training.

## COMPETITOR AD MESSAGING PATTERNS

### Pattern 1: FOMO / Invisibility (Most Common)
> "Your competitors are being recommended by ChatGPT. You're not."
- Used by: Nimt.ai, Briljant, BabyLoveGrowth, AutoSEO
- Works because: Creates urgency. Business owners don't know they're invisible.

### Pattern 2: Fire Your Agency (Price Disruption)
> "Stop paying $5,000/month for agencies"
- Used by: AutoSEO, RankPill
- Works because: Everyone hates paying agency fees for unclear results.

### Pattern 3: Autopilot / Sleep (Passive Income)
> "Grow traffic while you sleep"
- Used by: RankPill, Outrank, BabyLoveGrowth, Soogle
- Works because: Promises zero effort.

### Pattern 4: Agency Revenue Stream (B2B)
> "A big revenue stream for your small agency"
- Used by: HikeSEO, Climbo, Briljant
- Works because: Agencies always looking for new services to resell.

### MentionedIn.ai Should Use: Pattern 1 + Pattern 4 Combined
> "Your clients are invisible in ChatGPT. Sell them the fix — white-labeled under YOUR brand."
