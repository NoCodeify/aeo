## Key Findings for Video

### What Gets Brands Mentioned

1. **Category leadership** - #1-3 in space always appear
2. **Clear positioning** - "Best for X" framing wins
3. **Review site presence** - G2, Clutch, Gartner citations help
4. **Recent awards/press** - Gemini pulls from recent rankings
5. **Consumer-facing** - B2C > B2B visibility

### What Makes Brands Invisible

1. **B2B-only business models** - Stripe, Square (APIs)
2. **Niche specialists** - Law firms, specific clinics
3. **"Me too" positioning** - Smaller DTC without differentiation
4. **Service marketplaces** - Thumbtack, TaskRabbit, Handy
5. **No content marketing** - Agencies without thought leadership
