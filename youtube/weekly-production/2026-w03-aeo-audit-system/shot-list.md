## A-Roll Shots (Talking Head)

### Shot 1: Hook
**Duration:** 0:00 - 0:45
**Energy Level:** HIGH
**Notes:** Confident, engaging. Lean in slightly on "10 different answers." Pause after "Step 2 takes 30 seconds..."

### Shot 2: Setup
**Duration:** 0:45 - 2:15
**Energy Level:** Medium-High
**Notes:** Personal story about hair clinic - be genuine. Frustrated energy on "what most people do." Build tension toward "You have no idea."

### Shot 3: Step 1 - Intake
**Duration:** 2:15 - 3:45
**Energy Level:** Medium
**Notes:** Teaching mode. Emphasize "Dream Queries" as the key phrase.

### Shot 4: Step 2 - First 50 Words
**Duration:** 3:45 - 5:45
**Energy Level:** Medium-High
**Notes:** Slightly judgmental when reading the "fail" example. Satisfied when reading the "pass" example.

### Shot 5: Step 3 - 10-Run Test
**Duration:** 5:45 - 7:45
**Energy Level:** HIGH
**Notes:** This is the "big reveal" - energy should match. Emphasize "Different answer. Every. Single. Time." Add empathy with "losing the coin flip" story.

### Shot 6: Step 4 - Discovery Mapping
**Duration:** 7:45 - 9:00
**Energy Level:** Medium
**Notes:** Strategic, insightful. Funnel metaphor should feel like a revelation. "The money is in discovery queries" should land.

### Shot 7: Step 5 - Playbook
**Duration:** 9:00 - 10:00
**Energy Level:** Medium-High
**Notes:** Building to climax. Satisfied when mentioning "zero to 8 out of 10."

### Shot 8: Payoff
**Duration:** 10:00 - 10:45
**Energy Level:** Medium-High
**Notes:** Bringing it home. Confident. "Systems beat hope" should be delivered with conviction.

### Shot 9: CTA
**Duration:** 10:45 - 11:15
**Energy Level:** Medium
**Notes:** Direct but not pushy. Genuine excitement about next video.

## Filming Schedule

### Day 1: Talking Head
**Time:** 2-3 hours
**Location:** Home office / studio

1. Set up equipment (30 min)
2. Test audio/video (10 min)
3. Warm up (practice hook 2-3 times)
4. Record in sections:
   - Hook (do 3 takes)
   - Setup (2 takes)
   - Step 1 (2 takes)
   - Step 2 (2 takes)
   - Step 3 (3 takes - important section)
   - Step 4 (2 takes)
   - Step 5 (2 takes)
   - Payoff (2 takes)
   - CTA (2 takes)

### Day 2: Screen Recordings
**Time:** 1 hour

1. Set up screen recording software
2. Clean browser
3. Record ChatGPT demos:
   - 10-run consistency demo (run same query multiple times)
   - Brand name search example
4. Export at 1080p

## Edit Notes

**Pacing:**
- Hook: Fast cuts, high energy
- Setup: Build tension, slow down before "You have no idea"
- Steps 1-5: Steady pace, insert B-roll every 10-15 seconds
- Step 3: Fastest pacing - emphasize the drama of different results
- Payoff: Slow down for impact
- CTA: Direct, clean

**Transitions:**
- Use simple cuts between sections
- J-cuts for B-roll (audio leads visual)
- No fancy transitions - clean and professional

**Text On Screen:**
- Step numbers (STEP 1, STEP 2, etc.) as lower thirds
- Key phrases: "Dream Queries", "First 50 Words", "10-Run Test"
- WHO/WHAT/WHERE/PRICE labels during Step 2
