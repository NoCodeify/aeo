## A-ROLL SECTIONS (Talking Head)

Record each section separately for clean editing.

### SECTION 1: HOOK
**Duration:** ~45 seconds
**Energy:** High, punchy, confident
**Key lines to nail:**
- "ChatGPT convinced me to buy a Porsche instead of a BMW"
- "If AI can change my mind on a 35 thousand dollar car..."

**Takes needed:** 2-3
**Notes:** This is the most important section. Get multiple takes. Energy must be HIGH from first word.

---

### SECTION 2: SETUP - Car History
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Energy:** Conversational, storytelling
**Key lines:**
- "I started with a BMW 320i GT"
- "Then I upgraded to a 6 Series"
- "I knew exactly what I wanted next. A BMW Z4."

**Takes needed:** 1-2
**Notes:** Natural, like telling a friend. Don't rush the car names.

---

### SECTION 3: SETUP - ChatGPT Conversation
**Duration:** ~45 seconds
**Energy:** Building intrigue
**Key lines:**
- "And I thought... why not ask it about cars?"
- "A Porsche Boxster."
- "At first, I pushed back."

**Takes needed:** 2-3
**Notes:** Pause before "A Porsche Boxster" for dramatic effect. The reveal should feel like a surprise.

---

### SECTION 4: SETUP - The Purchase
**Duration:** ~25 seconds
**Energy:** Satisfied, slightly surprised at yourself
**Key lines:**
- "I bought the Porsche."
- "A 987 Boxster 3.2S. About 35 thousand dollars."
- "But that's not why I'm telling you this story."

**Takes needed:** 1-2
**Notes:** Small smile when saying "I bought the Porsche." Last line should create curiosity.

---

### SECTION 5: POINT 1 - The Realization
**Duration:** ~90 seconds
**Energy:** Building intensity, making the connection
**Key lines:**
- "The moment I drove that Porsche home, something clicked."
- "What is it doing to YOUR customers?"
- "I asked an AI. And I trusted its answer."

**Takes needed:** 2-3
**Notes:** This is the pivot to business. Lean in slightly when talking about "YOUR customers."

---

### SECTION 6: POINT 2 - The Problem
**Duration:** ~90 seconds
**Energy:** Empathetic, then concerning
**Key lines:**
- "You've spent years optimizing for Google"
- "They're invisible."
- "I call this the AEO problem."

**Takes needed:** 2-3
**Notes:** "They're invisible" should land heavy. Pause before "AEO problem."

---

### SECTION 7: POINT 3 - Why Different
**Duration:** ~60 seconds
**Energy:** Authoritative, contrarian confidence
**Key lines:**
- "They're wrong."
- "They ARE the answer."
- "Or are you invisible?"

**Takes needed:** 2
**Notes:** "They're wrong" should feel confident, not aggressive. This is the climax.

---

### SECTION 8: PAYOFF
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Energy:** Reflective, then forward-looking
**Key lines:**
- "That Porsche sitting in my garage is a daily reminder."
- "This isn't the future. This is happening right now."

**Takes needed:** 1-2
**Notes:** Slower pace here. Let the realization land.

---

### SECTION 9: CTA
**Duration:** ~30 seconds
**Energy:** Direct, helpful, action-oriented
**Key lines:**
- "Here's what I want you to do."
- "I'll see you in that video."

**Takes needed:** 1-2
**Notes:** Clear and confident. Point to where end screen will be.
