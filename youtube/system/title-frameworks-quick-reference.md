## üéØ 5 HIGH-POWER FORMULAS (For Immediate Use)

### Formula 1: Authority + Time + Specificity
**Structure**: `[Expert Title] Shows How to [Specific Goal] in [Short Time]`
**Example**: "Claude Engineer Reveals How to Build Scalable Agents in 7 Days"
**Target Hook Score**: 1,500-2,500
**Best For**: Educational, tutorial, how-to

### Formula 2: Problem + Scale + Emotional
**Structure**: `[Metric]% of [Profession] Don't Know [Problem] (Costs $$$)`
**Example**: "99% of Startups Don't Know This AI Pattern (Costs Millions)"
**Target Hook Score**: 2,000-3,500
**Best For**: Problem-awareness, pain-point targeting

### Formula 3: Contradiction + Time + Proof
**Structure**: `The [Counterintuitive Solution] (I Did It In [Time])`
**Example**: "The Simplest Multi-Agent Architecture (I Shipped It in 2 Weeks)"
**Target Hook Score**: 2,000-3,000
**Best For**: Differentiation, simplicity angle, case studies

### Formula 4: Question + Discovery + Urgency
**Structure**: `What [Authority] Won't Tell You About [Topic] (In [Year])`
**Example**: "What Anthropic Won't Tell You About Agent Design (In 2026)"
**Target Hook Score**: 1,500-2,500
**Best For**: Insider knowledge, discovery, trending

### Formula 5: Extreme + Proof + Emotional
**Structure**: `I [Extreme Action] to [Goal]. Here's What Changed.`
**Example**: "I Built 10 AI Systems from Scratch. Here's the Pattern."
**Target Hook Score**: 2,500-4,000
**Best For**: Personal stories, transformation, credibility

## ‚úÖ WHAT ALWAYS WORKS (Proven High Scorers)

- **Lists** (5, 10, 12 items) + Time-specific detail
- **Time-based goals** (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, 1 year)
- **Specific metrics** (99%, $10K/month, 100K subscribers)
- **Direct audience call-out** ("If you're X...")
- **Problem + cost** ("Costs millions," "Wastes hours")
- **Authority positioning** (Expert, Engineer, CEO explains)
- **Contradiction** (Simple vs. complex; lazy vs. productive)
- **Emotional triggers** (Regret, shame, FOMO, desire)
- **Current year/trend marker** ("In 2026," "Before 2025")

---

## ‚ùå WHAT NEVER WORKS

- Generic educational titles ("Tutorial," "How-to," "Guide")
- No specific audience identification
- Vague benefit ("This will change your life" without specifics)
- No time/urgency element
- No problem framing
- Pure feature listing
- No emotional hook
- Missing specificity (too broad)
