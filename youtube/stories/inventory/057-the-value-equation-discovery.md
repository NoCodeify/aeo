**Context:** Studying premium clinic pricing  
**The Realization:**
- Turkey: $2-3K
- Local clinics: $5-8K  
- Premium US: $15-20K
- Same FUE technique, same tools, same recovery
- Only difference: Positioning
- People don't pay for products, they pay for transformations
- Four factors: Dream Outcome, Likelihood of Success, Time Delay, Effort/Sacrifice
