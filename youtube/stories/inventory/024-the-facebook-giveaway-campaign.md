**Timeline:** Summer 2020  
**Key Details:**
- Started with "Win a FREE Hair Transplant" campaign
- $20/day Facebook ad spend
- Munib's panic: "Twenty dollars EVERY DAY? And we're giving away a procedure for FREE?"
- Results:
  - Leads at less than $1 each
  - Hundreds of entries within days
  - Converted non-winners into consultations
- First month revenue: $26,488
- Total cost: ~$200 in ads + one free procedure
- Quote: "Now we spend $1,000 per day and laugh about how scared we were of $20"
