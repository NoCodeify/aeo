**Simple Rule:** "Are we fully booked? Yes? Raise prices"
**Application:**
- Every time hit capacity, raised prices
- Got MORE bookings, not less
- 12-month waitlist at $100K = still underpriced
- No complex pricing models
- Just basic economics
- Let market tell you what you're worth
