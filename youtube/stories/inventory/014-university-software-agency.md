**Timeline:** During computer engineering studies  
**Team:** Sohaib + 3 friends (5 team members total)  
**Key Details:**
- First venture into legitimate service business
- First project: Big software development job worth $5,000
- Split among 5 team members = $1,000 each
- Time to complete: Almost ONE YEAR
- Actual earnings: Less than $100 per month per person
- Reflection: "It was halal money. Real service, real value, real learning"
- No gray areas or questionable business models
- Lesson learned: "The learning is valuable, the experience is crucial, but the money? Don't quit your day job yet"
- Problems discovered:
  - Landing projects as students with no reputation was hard
  - Spent months pitching before landing first project
  - Splitting money 5 ways made numbers tough
