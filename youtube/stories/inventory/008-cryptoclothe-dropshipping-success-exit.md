**Timeline:** 2016-2017  
**Key Details:**
- Pivoted from FBA to dropshipping
- Combined two trends: dropshipping + crypto
- Created "CryptoClothe" - crypto-themed apparel
- First month revenue: €15,000
- Problems encountered:
  - Shipping times: 3-4 weeks from China
  - Constant quality complaints
  - Chargebacks when items arrived late
  - Hundreds of competitors copying same products
- Exit: Sold CryptoClothe for €10,000 after 6 months
- Reflection: "Better than losing money, but for the stress and time invested? Barely minimum wage"
