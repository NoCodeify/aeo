**Purpose:** Transferring skills without quality loss  
**Process:**
1. **Document** - Write down how you do things
2. **Demonstrate** - Show them while following the document
3. **Duplicate** - Have them do it while you watch
**Result:** Systematic skill transfer that maintains standards
