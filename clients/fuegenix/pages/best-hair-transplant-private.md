## How to Choose a Private Hair Transplant Clinic

*What separates genuine privacy from marketing claims.*

When evaluating clinics for discretion, consider these factors in order of importance:

**Physical infrastructure.** Does the clinic operate a single-patient model, or do they simply offer a private room within a busy facility? A VIP room is not privacy. Single-patient scheduling is privacy.

**Communication protocols.** Are consultations and follow-ups conducted through encrypted channels? Is your file stored under enhanced data protection? Can the clinic guarantee that your name never appears in any system accessible to non-clinical staff?

**Location and access.** Is the clinic in a high-traffic area where you might be recognised? Is the entrance visible from public streets? Are arrival and departure times coordinated to prevent overlap?

**Referral networks.** Do they advertise publicly, or do clients arrive through private referrals? Clinics that rely on social media marketing are fundamentally less discreet than those that operate through word-of-mouth.
